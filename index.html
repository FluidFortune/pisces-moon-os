WELCOME TO PISCES MOON OS
A working demo of a general-purpose OS for the LilyGO T-Deck Plus — a $50 handheld computer built on the ESP32-S3. 47 apps. Dual-core architecture. Built in one month.
HOW TO NAVIGATE
Tap any category to open it.
Tap an app to launch it.
Tap the green header bar at the top to exit any app and go back.
That's how the real device works too.
GEMINI AI TERMINAL
Open INTEL → TERMINAL to chat with Google's Gemini AI — the same AI that runs on the real device.

You'll need a free API key from Google. No credit card required. Get one at:
aistudio.google.com/apikey

The key is stored in your browser only. It never touches our servers.
CONNECT YOUR ESP32 DEVICE
Have an ESP32-S3 running Pisces Moon OS? The Bridge App lets you connect your device via USB and drive the demo with live real data.

When connected, these apps use live device data: WarDrive, GPS, BT Radar, Net Scanner, RF Spectrum, Packet Sniffer, Hash Tool, Beacon Spotter — and the AI Terminal sends prompts to your device's Gemini client directly.

⚠ Bridge App connectivity coming to the repo on or before May 5, 2026.
github.com/FluidFortune/PiscesMoon
GHOST ENGINE
Watch the thin bar just below the header after launch. That's the Ghost Engine — a simulated background task running on Core 0, continuously scanning for WiFi networks and BLE devices while you use other apps on Core 1.

On the real device, this runs literally in the background while you play chess, write notes, or chat with the AI. Dual-core, always on.
